About us

Overlord Technology is a tech business with branches all over South Africa and Asia. we pride ourselves by selling high quality technology Acquired from our varies Partners which provide us with all types of technology from around the world.

Overlord Technology has led the digital transformation of the IT industry in South Africa, by being selected as the chosen partner of the worlds largest brands, HP, Dell, Lenovo, Microsoft, Logitech to name a few.

We are able to deliver on our unique offering through the use of our own marketplace technology supported by Lockstok to give our partners the ability to manage product listing, pricing, stock availability and orders all within a single platform.
